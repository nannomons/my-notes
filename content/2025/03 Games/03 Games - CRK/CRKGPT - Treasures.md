# 🔥 S-Tier Treasure Rankings (Arena Focused)

|Rank|Treasure|Type|Key Strengths|Primary Use Case|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|🥇 1|**Old Pilgrim's Scroll**|Epic|Massive universal ATK boost, works with every team comp.|All game modes (esp. PvP)|
|🥈 2|**Dream Conductor's Whistle**|Epic|CRIT% boost, ATK & DMG Resist to top 2 DPS, team heal on their death.|Arena dual-DPS comps|
|🥉 3|**Sleepyhead's Jelly Watch**|Epic|Cooldown + ATK buff, top-tier tempo tool once maxed.|High-level PvP/PvE teams|
|4|**Squishy Jelly Watch**|Common|Easier to max, strong CD reduction, essential from early to endgame.|PvP, PvE, Guild Battle|
|5|**Sugar Swan's Shining Feather**|Epic|One-time revive with full heal + Skill reset. Great comeback potential.|Arena (esp. burst teams)|
|6|**Explorer's Monocle**|Epic|Cleanse + heal + DEF & Debuff Resist buff, short CD, versatile support.|PvP (anti-debuff comps)|
|7|**Librarian's Enchanted Robes**|Epic|ATK SPD boost to top 2 SPD Cookies, great in combo/auto-attack teams.|Arena (combo/ATK SPD comps)|
|8|**Grim-looking Scythe**|Rare|Boosts CRIT Chance for team, high damage ceiling, good synergy with CRIT builds.|PvP burst comps|
|9|**Mysterious Jewelry Box**|Epic|AoE damage + unremovable debuffs (DEF-, ATK-, CRIT-), strong CC/disruptor tool.|PvP burst/control teams|

# ⚔️ Notes:

- **Top 3** (Scroll, Whistle, Jelly Watch) are staples across almost all modes.
- **Feather** and **Jewelry Box** shine in **comeback or burst strategies**.
- **Explorer’s Monocle** is the **best anti-debuff Treasure**.
- **Enchanted Robes** and **Grim Scythe** work best in **speed/crit-based builds**.
