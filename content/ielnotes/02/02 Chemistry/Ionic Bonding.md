A type of linkage formed from the **electrostatic attraction** between **oppositely charged ions in chemical [[Compounds]]**. Such a bond forms when the [[Valence Electrons]] of one atom are transferred permanently to another atom. The atom that **loses** the electrons becomes a **positively charged ion** ([[Cations]]), while the one that **gains** them becomes a **negatively charged ion** ([[Anions]]).
