## Types of Arguments
1. **[[Deductive Argument]]:** Piecing together true statements to arrive at a valid conclusion. General to specific.
2. **[[Inductive Argument]]:** Using past experiences to arrive at a valid conclusion. Specific to general.
3. **[[Abductive Argument]]:** Providing a plausible explanation after the facts.
