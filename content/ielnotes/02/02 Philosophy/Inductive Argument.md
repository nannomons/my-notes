**==Induction== or inductive arguments** uses **past experiences to make future predictions**. 
- A.k.a.: Educated guess.
- E.g. you know that a hot pan on a stove will burn you if you touch it. 

> [!EXAMPLE]
> - **Premise 1:** Most men in ancient Athens had beards.
> - **Premise 2:** Socrates was a man who lived in ancient Athens.
> - **Conclusion:** Socrates probably had a beard.
