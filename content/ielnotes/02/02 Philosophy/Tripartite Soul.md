The "self" is divided into three aspects. 
1. **The Rational:** Logical; how we are persuaded by science and analytics.
2. **The Spirited:** Emotional; how we feel and how those feelings affect the way we act.
3. **The Appetitive:** Physical Desires; the primitive carnal instincts of humans that we share with all animals.

![[Tripartite Soul.png]]

 [[Plato]] believed that humans were ruled by the **rational part of the soul**, which set us apart from the **appetitive** beasts.
