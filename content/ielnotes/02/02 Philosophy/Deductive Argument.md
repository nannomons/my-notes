[[Entailment]]: If A is true, B must follow. A necessarily leads to B.

> [!EXAMPLE]
> Example:
> - **Premise A:** All humans are mortal.
> - **Premise B:** Socrates is human.
> - **Conclusion:** Socrates is mortal. 

This ensures that if the premises are true, then the conclusions are true.
