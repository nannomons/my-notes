case in point, if the problem asks for a **Right Reimann Approximation**, the lines segmenting the *delta points* (e.g., at $x=(0,2,4)$) must be the *right vertices* of the rectangles of which we will compute the area. if **Left Reimann Approximation**, then $x$ should be the *left vertices*. 

after computing the area of each segment, add them to get the *Reimann Sum*.
