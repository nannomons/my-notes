Hi! A **piecewise function** is any function that has two or more "pieces," or in other words, *fucked up*! Hope this helps!

Here's an example from [[Limits and Continuity]]:
$$
g(x) = \begin{cases}
x^2, & x \ne 2 \\
1, & x = 2
\end{cases}
$$
