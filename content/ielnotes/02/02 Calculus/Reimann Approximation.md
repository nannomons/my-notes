The **Reimann Approximation** is the *approximation* of the area under a curve using some rectangles. Also known as the [[02/02 Calculus/Reimann Sum]]. They can be categorized between *[[underestimations]]* or *[[overestimations]]*, and *right* and *left* Reimann sums.
