**Integral calculus** is the way we try to quantify or approximate an infinite number as we take the limit as n approaches infinity. It's main thing is *measuring area under a curve*. It's called *integral* calculus because the central operation used is the integral or summation. [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/integral-calculus/).
