**Commutativity** means that $a$ and $b$ can be switched around during addition and multiplication.

**For addition:** For every $a$ and $b$ in $\mathbb{R}$, $a+b=b+a$.
**For multiplication:** For every $a$ and $b$ in $\mathbb{R}$, $a*b=b*a$.
