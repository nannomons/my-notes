Any math statement that *can be shown as true* using accepted logical or mathematical arguments. 

*Other forms of theorems* include:
- [[Corollary]], the "special cases" in theorems. 
- [[Proposition]], the less important theorems but are still worth writing down, and
- [[Lemma]], the unimportant facts but are essential in proving the main theorems.
