# Cancellation Law for Addition PROOF (real not fake)
Then, 
	$a+b=a+c$. 
	$a+b+(-a)=a+c+(-a)$ ; [[Axioms]]: APE
	$a+(b+(-a))=a+(c+(-a))$ ; [[Axioms]]: Associativity for Addition
	$a+((-a)+b)=(a+(-a))+c$ ; [[Axioms]]: Commutativity
	$(a+(-a))+b=(a+(-a))+c$ ; [[Axioms]]: Associativity
	$0+b=0+c$ ; [[Axioms]]: Ex. of Additive Inverse
	$b=c$ ; [[Axioms]]: Ex. of Additive Identity
