If $ab=0$ then $a=0$ OR $b=0$.

Case 1: If $a-0$ then *the statement is true*.
Case 2: If $a\neq 0$, then 
	$a \neq 0$
	$a\left( \frac{1}{a} \right)=1$ ; Existence of Additive Inverse

Now, by assumption, $ab=0$
	$ab\left( \frac{1}{a} \right)=0\left( \frac{1}{a} \right)$ ; MPE
	$a\left(b \frac{1}{a} \right)=0$ ; Associativity
	$a(\left( \frac{1}{a} \right)b)$ ; Commutativity
	$(a \frac{1}{a})b=0$ ; Associativity
	$1b=0$ ; Existence of Multiplicative Inverse
	$b=0$ ; Existence of Multiplicative Identity
