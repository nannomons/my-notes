**Codominance** is a form of inheritance where two alleles of the same gene are *expressed roughly equal amounts* in an organism, meaning *both phenotypes are displayed*.

This does not follow [[Mendel's Laws of Inheritance]] and thus is also called a [[Non-Mendelian Inheritance]].
