**Polygenic inheritance** is a type of inheritance in which *multiple genes control the phenotype of an organism*. Also known as **quantitative inheritance** or **multifactorial inheritance**. This includes *height or eye color*, which is determined by the cumulative effects of many genes. 

Contrasts [[Monogenic Inheritance]] in which one singular gene controls the outcome.
