**Metabolism**, the sum of all *chemical reactions* in an organism.
- **Catabolism:** 
	- Cat sounds like cut—breaking down molecules to release energy
- **Anabolism:** Building molecules to store energy

![[Pasted image 20250908164847.png]] 

We catabolize bigger units into smaller units to be easily broken down and absorbed by the body, to be used to build materials that our body needs such as energy by the process of anabolism.
