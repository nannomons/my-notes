**Evolution** is the process by which animals, plants, and other living organisms are transformed into different forms by the accumulation of changes over successive generations. In other words, *the change in [[Allele Frequency]] in a population over time*. 

> Change is the only constant in the world.

**[[Natural Selection]]**: The more advantageous and beneficial traits, the more likely to survive.  
**[[Adaptation]]**: Inherited traits improves and organisms ability to survive and reproduce.
