A **monohybrid cross** is a genetic cross that examines the inheritance of a single trait. 

Ex.: One variation: Flower color. A monohybrid cross would involve crossing a plant with red flowers (RR) with a plant with white flowers (rr). The offspring or F1 generation will all have dominant traits (red flowers; RR)
