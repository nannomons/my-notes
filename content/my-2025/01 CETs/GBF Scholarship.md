# Requirements
Submission of Application and Requirements is open until MAY 30, 2025.

1. Proof of Academic Standing
🔳 For Incoming College Students:
▫ Certified True Copy of Grades – Grade 12 Report Card (1st term only if 2nd term is not yet available)
▫ Notice of Admission or Proof of University Application for AY 2025-2026

🔳 For Continuing College Students (2nd year and above):
▫ Certified True Copy of Grades – 1st Term AY 2024-2025 (Signed/Certified portal-generated grades are accepted)
▫ Registration Form – 2nd/3rd Term AY 2024-2025

2. Proof of Annual Household Income (Submit any of the following documents)
🔳 If parent(s) are employed (private/public sector) or self-employed:
▫ Recently filed 2024 Annual Income Tax Return (ITR): Form 2303 (employed) or Form 1701 (self-employed)
▫ Certificate of Employment with Salary (dated 2025) for the parent contributing to household income
▫ Employment Contract with Salary (for Overseas Filipino Workers - OFWs)
▫ Screenshot of January-March 2025 income (for self-employed individuals in ride-hailing services like Grab/Lalamove)

🔳 If the family's income depends on a senior parent’s pension:
▫ Proof of Pension

🔳 If the parent(s) do not have a regular source of income and are exempt from filing taxes:
▫ BIR Tax Exemption Certificate (To be submitted later upon successful application.)

3. Proof of Billing
▫ Electricity or Water Bill matching the declared address of residence (dated 2025)

4. Recommender's email address (Must not be from immediate family members or relatives)
▫ One (1) from a Guidance Counselor
▫ One (1) from any of the following: Adviser, Professor, Principal, School Head, or College Dean
