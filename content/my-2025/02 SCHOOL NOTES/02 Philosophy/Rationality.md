**==Rationality==** is our **ability to reason and be persuaded** by [[Arguments]]. 

According to [[Aristotle]], **==rationality==** is our distinguishing characteristic—what sets us apart from the beasts. 

According to [[Plato]], **==rationality==** is the leading aspect of the [[Tripartite Soul]].
