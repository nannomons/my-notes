## 🔥 THE LAZY GIRL MUSCLE PLAN: ARMS & BACK EDITION

### 🥚 1. **Nutrition for Muscle Gain (Without Complicating Things)**

If you eat mostly **eggs and chicken**, you're _already halfway there_. Just make sure:

- **Protein intake goal**: 1.2–1.6g per kg of body weight daily.  
    (If you're ~55 kg, aim for ~66–88g protein/day)
    
- **Simple meal structure**:
    
    - Breakfast: 2–3 eggs + rice or bread
    - Lunch: chicken breast + rice or sweet potato
    - Dinner: same as lunch
    - Snacks: peanut butter, cheese, yogurt, or protein shake (optional but helpful)

> ⚠️ You can gain muscle with minimal variety as long as your protein is high and you're eating _enough_. Don't under-eat.

---

### 🏋️‍♀️ 2. **The Lazy Workout Routine (15–20 mins, 3–4x/week)**

**No gym? No problem. You can start at home.**

#### 💪 Main Muscles: Arms + Back = Pulling & Pushing

Alternate 2 workouts every other day:

---

#### **Workout A: Arm & Back Focus (Pulling)**

- **Wall Pulls / Rows** (use towel or resistance band) – 3x10
    
- **Back Extensions** (on bed or mat) – 3x12
    
- **Bicep curls** (water bottles or bags) – 3x10
    
- **Dead Hangs** (grab a doorframe or bar if possible) – 3x30 sec
    

> _Start light. Just focus on form and feeling the muscle work._

---

#### **Workout B: Push & Core (Arm/Shoulder Shape)**

- **Knee or Wall Push-ups** – 3x10
    
- **Overhead Press (water bottles)** – 3x10
    
- **Tricep Dips (on chair)** – 3x10
    
- **Plank** – 3x20–30 sec
    

---

### 🧠 3. **Mindset Hack: Make It Stupid Easy**

- Put your mat or dumbbells **where you can see them**.
    
- **Track with a checklist** — 3 workouts/week = ✅✅✅
    
- Play a **LeanBeefPatty YouTube video** while working out for inspiration.
    
- Reward yourself (e.g., dessert, YouTube break) _only_ after finishing.
    

---

### 🧃 4. **Supplements? Optional, But Helpful**

- **Whey protein**: If you can’t eat enough, 1 scoop = ~25g protein.
    
- **Creatine monohydrate**: Safe and helps strength (3–5g/day).
